# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer retention and churn behavior using the Telco Customer Churn dataset. The objective is to identify customer segments with high churn risk, understand retention patterns, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer loyalty and reduce customer attrition.

Developed as part of **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Task 2 (2026)**.

## Objectives

* Analyze customer churn trends and retention patterns
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Explore the impact of contract types, payment methods, and internet services on churn
* Generate business recommendations to improve customer retention

## Tools Used

* Power BI

* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

* Total Customers

## Key Insights

* Month-to-Month contract customers exhibit the highest churn rate.
* Customers using Electronic Check payments are more likely to churn.
* Fiber Optic users show higher churn compared to other internet service groups.
* Customers with longer tenure demonstrate stronger retention behavior.

## Business Recommendations

1. Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
2. Improve onboarding experiences for new customers.
3. Promote automatic payment methods.
4. Address service quality concerns among Fiber Optic users.
5. Implement targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers.

## Dataset

Telco Customer Churn Dataset from Kaggle.
